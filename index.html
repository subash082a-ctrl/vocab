        Install and use on mobile

        1. Open this page from your phone browser.
        2. Tap the install prompt or add to home screen.
        3. Use the tabs to explore search, categories, and quiz practice.
        4. Save words from the home screen and review them later.

        When installed, the app works like a normal phone app and is available offline after the first load.
